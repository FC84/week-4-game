Crystal Collector

How to play the game


  1. You will be assigned a random number at the start.
  2. Clicking a Crystal will add to your total score
  3. You win by matching your current score to the random number.
  4. You lose the game if your current score goes above the random number.
  5. The value of each crystal is hidden until clicked.
  6. Each values are generated at random during each game instance.
